About

I am a researcher interested in the field of modeling human visual perception mechanism for artificial intelligence. The main research interests include low-level vision and deep learning. I am currently working as a research engineer at LG Electronics, Korea. I received my Ph.D. degree in engineering at Yonsei University, Korea. I spent some time as a postdoctoral researcher at Yonsei University and a member of Multimedia Computing and Machine Learning (MCML) Group led by Prof. Jong-Seok Lee.
